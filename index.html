Power electronics circuits for beginner

Advantages and Disadvantages of circuits??

Figure 1: Simple Switch with Inductor

Advantage: Very simple and low-cost design.

Disadvantage: When the switch turns off, the inductor generates a high voltage spike (back EMF) that can damage the switch.

Figure 2: Switch with Inductor, Resistor, and Diode

Advantage: The diode provides a path for the inductor current when the switch turns off, protecting the switch from voltage spikes. The resistor helps dissipate the stored energy safely.

Disadvantage: The resistor causes energy loss as heat, reducing overall efficiency.

Figure 3: Push-Pull or H-Bridge Configuration

Advantage: Allows for bidirectional current flow and better control of the inductor. The diodes help recover energy, improving efficiency and protecting the switches.
